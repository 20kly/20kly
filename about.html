About the Game

"20,000 Light Years Into Space" was written for Pyweek (March 2006) by Jack Whitham. It is a single-player real-time strategy game with a science-fiction theme. For game updates, visit www.jwhitham.org.uk.

About the Author

Jack Whitham is a second-year PhD student at The University of York, specialising in embedded real-time systems. "20,000 Light Years" is Jack's first entry into a games competition, and his first real-time strategy game.
